# Credits & Acknowledgments

MiniMax Skills builds upon ideas and work from the open-source community. We are grateful to the following projects and authors whose contributions helped shape skills in this repository.

## frontend-dev

The design engineering framework in `frontend-dev` — including the design-variance system, motion recipes, card archetypes, color rules, and forbidden-pattern checklist — is derived from and inspired by:

- **[taste-skill](https://github.com/Leonxlnx/taste-skill)** by [Leonxlnx (Leon Lin)](https://github.com/Leonxlnx) — Anti-Slop Frontend design engineering skill framework.

MiniMax extended the original framework with MiniMax API asset generation, copywriting modules (AIDA/PAS/FAB), p5.js generative art workflows, multi-framework support, accessibility guidelines, and additional motion presets.
